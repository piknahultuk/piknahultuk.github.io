About

The thinking behind the work

I'm a Computer Engineering student at Erciyes University working as both a designer and a developer — treating the two as one craft rather than separate disciplines. Every product I make is shaped equally by how it's built and how it feels.


On the design side I work with Figma, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop, focusing on layout, typography and interaction. On the development side I build with React, React Native, Firebase, and Node.js. Neither leads — they inform each other at every step.


Currently growing as a designer-developer hybrid, open to internships, collaborations, and interesting challenges.
